Step 6: Post-Imputation QC & Dataset Finalization

Apply quality filters and remove heterozygosity outliers to create final analysis-ready dataset

✓ Completed — December 26, 2025

1. Overview

This final step applies stringent quality filters to the imputed dataset and removes heterozygosity outliers. The strategy keeps quality-filtered data for imputation, then removes problematic samples post-imputation after imputation has completed.

Strategy: Heterozygosity outliers were NOT removed before imputation (to preserve LD information for the imputation server), but are removed after imputation completion.

4. Commands Executed

Step 1: Identify heterozygosity outliers

plink --bfile imputed_merged --het --out heterozygosity

Step 2: Extract outliers (F > 0.2 or F < -0.2)

awk 'NR>1 && ($6 < -0.2 || $6 > 0.2) {print $1, $2}' heterozygosity.het > remove_het_outliers.txt

Step 3: Remove outlier samples

plink --bfile imputed_merged --remove remove_het_outliers.txt --make-bed --out UZB_imputed_HQ_clean
